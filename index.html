2014 - 2016

Gunmetal

We were an independent web design and development studio based in Sydney, Australia.

Gunmetal was a collaboration between Hayden Barnett and Hayden Bleasel. We had the privilege of working with clients such as Vimbra, Trekalo, Ryzer, Wild Animal Encounters and National Geographic Channel. We have since left to follow other opportunities, but feel free to contact us anytime:
